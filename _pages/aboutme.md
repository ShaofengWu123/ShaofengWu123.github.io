## Biography

Shaofeng Wu(伍少枫) is an undergraduate student majoring in Computer Science in <a href="http://en.ustc.edu.cn/">University of Science and Technology of China(USTC)</a>. His previous major is Materials Physics and he has been concentrated on clean energy technologies(HER, solar steaming) in his undergraduate research. He received his Bachelor degree of Natural Science in 2020. Driven by strong interest in programming and computer science topics, he continued his study in Computer Science in USTC as a Second-Bachelor student the same year. In 2022, he joined NetX group in CUHK as a PhD student supervised by Hong Xu, Henry. His current research direction is SmartNIC/DPU.
